BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN CIVIL ENGINEERING (BSCE)
 
 

PROGRAM OBJECTIVES

• To prepare for planning, design, construction, management, and maintenance of: railways, rods, and bridges: dams, reservoirs, and hydraulic structures and systems: water and wastewater disposal: buildings and foundation and: other types of civil work used by man.
• To be environmentally conscious.
• To be knowledgeable of their professional responsibilities on ethics and laws.
• To be knowledgeable on human, financial, physical, and resource management.
• To be totally equipped with the essential knowledge, professional competence, skills and values relevant to the needs and demands of the industry, academe and society.

PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

The Civil Engineering (CE) Program enables aspiring engineers to develop thinking, collaborative, and technical skills for solving complex civil engineering problems and to learn how to assimilate new knowledge needed for the design, construction, and maintenance of roads, bridges, buildings, water supply, irrigation, flood control, ports, and other infrastructure while considering impact in various contexts.
 

STUDENT ORGANIZATION

CIVIL ENGINEERING SOCIETY (CES)

The Civil Engineering Society (CES) at Technological University of the Philippines (TUP) Manila is dedicated to fostering professional growth and community among aspiring civil engineers. Through dynamic initiatives like seminars, workshops, and competitions, CES enriches theoretical learning with practical experiences, equipping members to excel in their field. CES also fosters camaraderie and mentorship through social events, creating a supportive environment for lasting connections. Beyond campus, CES engages in outreach programs and collaborations with industry, advocating for civil engineering's role in addressing societal needs. CES empowers future civil engineers to thrive professionally and contribute meaningfully to society.
