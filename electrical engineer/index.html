BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING (BSEE)
 
 

PROGRAM OBJECTIVES

• To provide the students an advanced knowledge various major in science and electrical engineering.
• To provide the students an in-depth understanding of the electrical engineering processes.
• To develop students' ability to integrate new computing tools in electrical engineering process.
• Create awareness of ethical issues in electrical engineering processes on ethical, moral and social issues in electrical engineering.
• To prepare electrical engineering graduates to conduct scientific research

PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

Electrical Engineering program deals with the study and use of technology and applied science involving electrical phenomena. It involves the application of the basic theories in the design, installation, operation, and maintenance of electrical apparatuses and systems as they are used in the generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization of electrical energy for various commercial, industrial, and other purposes.
 

STUDENT ORGANIZATION

TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING SOCIETY (TUPEES)

The Technological University of the Philippines Electrical Engineering Society (TUPEES) serves as the official student organization representing Electrical Engineering at TUP Manila, committed to fostering professional growth and community among aspiring electrical engineers. Through a diverse range of initiatives including seminars, workshops, and competitions, TUPEES enhances theoretical learning with practical experiences, while also fostering camaraderie and mentorship through various social events. Moreover, TUPEES extends its impact beyond campus through outreach programs and industry partnerships, advocating for the importance of electrical engineering in addressing societal challenges. Ultimately, TUPEES empowers future electrical engineers to excel and make meaningful contributions to society.
