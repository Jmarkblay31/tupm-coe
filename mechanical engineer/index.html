BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN MECHANICAL ENGINEERING (BSME)
 
 

PROGRAM OBJECTIVES

• To provide the students an advanced knowledge in various major in science and mechanical engineering.
• To provide the students an in-depth understanding of the mechanical engineering processes.
• To develop students' ability to integrate new computing tools in mechanical engineering processes.
• To create awareness among the faculty and students on ethical issues in mechanical engineering.
• To develop the students' ability to relate mechanical engineering to other disciplines.
• To prepare mechanical engineering graduates to conduct scientific research.

PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

Mechanical Engineering is a profession that concerns itself with mechanical design, energy conversion, fuel and combustion technologies, heating, ventilating, and air conditioning (HVAC), refrigeration systems, fire-protection systems, heat transfer, materials, noise control and acoustics, manufacturing processes, rail transportation, automatic control, product safety and reliability, solar energy, and technological impacts to society.
 

STUDENT ORGANIZATION

PHILIPPINE SOCIETY OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERS (PSME)

Philippine Society of Mechanical Engineers (PSME) is the official student organization for Mechanical Engineering at Technological University of the Philippines (TUP) Manila, dedicated to fostering professional growth and community among aspiring engineers. Through diverse initiatives like seminars, workshops, and competitions, PSME supplements theoretical learning with practical experiences, while also promoting camaraderie and mentorship through social events. Beyond campus, PSME engages in outreach programs and industry partnerships, advocating for the significance of mechanical engineering in addressing societal challenges. In essence, PSME at TUP empowers future engineers to excel and contribute meaningfully to society.
